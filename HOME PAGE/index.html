JEE

JEE" typically refers to the "Joint Entrance Examination," which is a set of competitive examinations in India for admission into various undergraduate engineering and architecture programs at top engineering institutes and universities. JEE is one of the most widely recognized and competitive entrance exams in the country.

NEET

The National Eligibility-cum-Entrance Test (NEET) is a competitive entrance examination in India for admission to undergraduate medical and dental courses in government and private medical colleges and dental colleges across the country.
